The Black Bar Image Dataset










What it is ?

The Black Bar Image Dataset, or "BBID", is a a prowerfull tool
dedicated to the development and assessment of computational models
It purpose is twofold :

- To serve in testing, as a standardized, parametrizable and reproducible tool for evaluating the performance of computational models.

- To aid in the developmental stage of computational models, allowing for clear evaluation of intermediate steps in the computing processes.
